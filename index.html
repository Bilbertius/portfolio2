my projects

JamCloud

Jamcloud was built to help fans of improv-centric music (often called 'jam band music') to track and share their favorite live recordings. I built it because I know all to well the massive task it is to try to accomplish this without help. Plans to add the ability for users' to share lists with friends as well as rate other users choices and see how their favorites stack up against a list comprised of jams with the highest average ratings. It was build using React, ExpressJS, and PostgreSQL.

Try-lingual

Try-lingual is an app that was built for students of foreign languages to help them learn and retain vocabulary. It utilizes spaced repetition to ensure the user is being quizzed on words it struggles with more frequently than those words which they find less challenging. The ultimate goal is to assist users in effectively and efficiently committing foreign vocab to memory. This app was built with React, ExpressJS, and PostgreSQL.

Indecisio

This app was built for anyone who has ever felt unsure of what to do with excess free time on their hands. It allows users to generate a random choice from a pool of all activities that have been submitted by other users. The user can decline activities until they see a choice that they like. Users can also narrow down the possible activities by selecting a category they wish for their activity to fall under. The user can also consult a list of previous activities that they have accepted as well as a global list of activities most commonly selected by other users. This app was built with React, ExpressJS, and PostgreSQL.
